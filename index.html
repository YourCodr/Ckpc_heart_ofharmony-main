About CKPC Heart of Harmony, Hosur Main Road Bengaluru

By CKPC · Authorised Channel Partner — Realhubb Ventures Pvt Ltd

Discover Harmony in the Heart of South Bengaluru — reserved for a discerning few.

Rising gracefully along Hosur Main Road (NH 48), just 10 minutes from the key Silk Board junction, CKPC Heart of Harmony is a thoughtfully crafted high-rise luxury enclave featuring 137 signature residences. Designed for the modern homebuyer seeking seamless connectivity to South Bengaluru's prime commercial corridors like Electronic City, HSR Layout, and Koramangala, this architectural masterpiece stands as a sanctuary of premium living.

Frequently Asked Questions

Where is Heart of Harmony located?+

CKPC Heart of Harmony is located on Hosur Main Road, South Bengaluru — about 10 minutes from Silk Board and 400 metres from Kudlu Gate Metro Station (Yellow Line).

What configurations are available?+

Heart of Harmony offers 137 signature residences across four configurations — 3 Bed Classic (1,786–1,824 sq.ft), 3 Bed Premium (1,981–2,184 sq.ft), 3 Bed + Study Luxe (2,132–2,189 sq.ft) and 4 Bed Grande (2,550–2,572 sq.ft).

What is the price of Heart of Harmony?+

Residences at Heart of Harmony are priced from ₹2.43 Cr to ₹3.81 Cr, all-inclusive and excluding Stamp Duty & Registration.

How many residences are in Heart of Harmony?+

Heart of Harmony is a single high-rise tower (2B+G+24 floors) on approximately 1.7 acres, comprising 137 signature residences.
